# Image Placeholder Instructions

Place your images in this directory:

- `profile.jpg` - Your profile/headshot photo (recommended: 350x350px)
- `project1.jpg` - Screenshot of your first project (recommended: 400x250px)
- `project2.jpg` - Screenshot of your second project
- `project3.jpg` - Screenshot of your third project
- `project4.jpg` - Screenshot of your fourth project
- `project5.jpg` - Screenshot of your fifth project
- `project6.jpg` - Screenshot of your sixth project

If images are not provided, placeholder images will be displayed automatically.

## Recommended Image Specifications

- **Profile Photo**: Square format (350x350px or larger), JPG or PNG
- **Project Screenshots**: Landscape format (400x250px or larger), JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Keep under 500KB for optimal loading speed
- **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for screenshots with transparency

## Image Optimization Tips

1. Compress images before uploading using tools like:
   - TinyPNG (https://tinypng.com)
   - Squoosh (https://squoosh.app)
   
2. Use descriptive filenames that match your projects
3. Consider using WebP format for better compression (requires updating image references in components)
